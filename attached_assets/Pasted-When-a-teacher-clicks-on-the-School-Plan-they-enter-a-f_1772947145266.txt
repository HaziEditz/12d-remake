When a teacher clicks on the School Plan, they enter a fully immersive, animated portal that guides them through the setup process. In this portal, teachers can create a custom payment quota for their school, generating a tailored quote that reflects the number of students and features the institution wants. Animated panels, sliding transitions, and interactive feedback make this process feel dynamic and engaging, while ensuring schools see the full value of the plan. It is important to note that the AI has not yet fully implemented this quota/payment system, so completing this functionality is critical for a seamless teacher onboarding experience. Unlike standard or casual accounts, students do not receive free trials, reinforcing that the School Plan is a premium, fully-featured educational environment.

Once students log in via their school join code, they enter a refined, visually immersive “school world” that is distinct from standard accounts. The layout is tailored to age group, with primary students seeing bright, playful visuals, and high school students experiencing sleek, sophisticated dashboards. Core pages — including the simulator, dashboard, assignments, and leaderboards — remain the same in functionality but are redesigned with custom CSS, animations, hover effects, and interactive panels, creating a polished and engaging environment. Students immediately notice a difference from standard accounts: animated charts, dynamic Fun Zone tiles, glowing hover interactions, and visually rewarding feedback on assignments and achievements make the learning experience immersive, motivating, and fun.

The simulator is fully customizable, allowing students to personalize chart colors, layouts, and themes to match their preferences while still tracking portfolios, investments, and historical market trends. The Casual Plan, blending features from the School Plan and 12Digits+, grants additional tools such as friend lists, collaborative challenges, and advanced portfolio analysis, while still keeping the immersive school-world aesthetic.

The Fun Zone is expanded into fully playable games, inspired by Banqer’s classroom economy mechanics, including earning virtual income, managing budgets, making investment decisions, and responding to simulated market events. Games also include additional interactive challenges like market prediction exercises, strategy puzzles (diversification, risk management, budgeting), and collaborative team-based portfolio growth competitions. Completing games and assignments rewards students with badges, streaks, and animated confetti effects, providing immediate visual feedback and gamified motivation.

On the teacher admin side, every student added via the join code is automatically included in a teacher-student group chat, allowing announcements, collaboration, and feedback in real-time. Teachers can monitor classroom performance, track Fun Zone engagement, manage assignments, and control the classroom economy, all within a visually engaging, animated interface.

Overall, this addition ensures that students experience a fully immersive, polished school world with distinct visuals, interactive dashboards, Fun Zone games, and a customizable simulator, while teachers have complete control over classrooms, student engagement, and subscription management. All transitions, animations, and interactive elements create a cohesive and engaging experience that surpasses standard platforms like Banqer. While the AI has implemented much of this, some features remain unfinished, and completing them is critical to ensure that the full immersive experience, functional games, join code system, group chat, and payment quota system operate flawlessly.
