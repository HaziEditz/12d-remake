12Digits School System – Immersive Separate World (Replit-Ready)

The 12Digits School System is designed as a fully separate, immersive environment within the main platform. When a school selects the School Plan or clicks the “Go to School System” button, the site transitions into a distinct, standalone world with its own interface, animations, and interactive experiences. This separate system feels like a completely new application, yet remains fully integrated with the main platform for user login, data tracking, and account management. The transition should include full-page animations — such as sliding, fading, or zooming effects — to visually convey the change from the main platform to the School System. The interface uses custom colors, styling, and graphics to reinforce the sense of entering a new, dedicated learning environment.

The onboarding process within the School System is fully animated and interactive. Teachers are greeted with a welcoming message, “Welcome to 12Digits – finance made easy for your students,” accompanied by smooth visual effects. As they scroll, different sections appear dynamically, showcasing all platform features including classroom portfolio simulations, interactive challenges, gamified lessons, Fun Zone mini-games, and teacher analytics dashboards. During onboarding, schools can access a custom payment and quote portal, where administrators can enter school name, admin name, email, and select a subscription plan. The system instantly generates a tailored quote, animates the form fields, and allows secure simulated payment. The payment portal is fully integrated into the animated onboarding sequence, giving administrators a professional, seamless experience without leaving the immersive environment.

Once the school is registered, teachers access a dedicated dashboard that controls classrooms, assignments, and the classroom economy. They can create assignments for students, set deadlines, and design scenario-based financial challenges such as portfolio growth targets, budgeting exercises, or investment simulations. Teachers can also trigger dynamic classroom events, such as market booms, downturns, or simulated economic news, which affect student investments in real-time. The dashboard should include visualizations of student progress, portfolio performance, and engagement metrics, allowing teachers to track results, identify students who need guidance, and manage their classroom economy effectively.

Students enter their own personalized dashboards, tailored by age group. The platform supports primary (ages 6–10), intermediate (ages 11–13), and high school (ages 14–18) levels. For primary students, dashboards are colorful, playful, and simplified, with basic lessons on coins, saving, and spending. Intermediate students experience a slightly more advanced interface, including basic investment concepts, team challenges, and medium-level Fun Zone games. High school students access advanced financial simulations, portfolio dashboards, scenario-based challenges, and complex gamified mini-games. Students can manage their virtual bank balances, make spending and investment decisions, and watch the results unfold dynamically through charts, animated updates, and visual feedback.

The School System features a fully gamified experience with achievements, badges, experience points, and leveling systems. Students earn rewards for completing lessons, challenges, and mini-games. Animated notifications, progress bars, and confetti effects celebrate achievements, maintaining engagement and motivation. Leaderboards display rankings by classroom, age group, and global comparisons, and are updated in real-time to reflect student progress. Students can see their accomplishments, streaks, and unlocked rewards, creating a sense of competition and fun while reinforcing learning outcomes.

The platform includes a Fun Zone, fully integrated into the School System world, offering mini-games and interactive challenges that teach financial skills through play. Games include team-based investment challenges, market prediction simulations, strategy puzzles on budgeting and diversification, and interactive quiz games. The Fun Zone adjusts automatically for age groups, ensuring younger students have simpler, visually engaging games while older students face complex, scenario-driven exercises. Rewards from the Fun Zone contribute to student levels, leaderboards, and achievement badges, creating a cohesive gamified experience.

Lessons within the School System are interactive and scenario-based. Each lesson includes animations, examples, and quizzes at the end to reinforce learning. Teachers can assign lessons and monitor completion, while students can interact with the content, make investment decisions in a sandbox environment, and receive immediate feedback. Tips and insights are integrated contextually, adapted for each age group, and provide real-world explanations of financial principles, market behavior, and risk management.

All aspects of the School System are designed to feel visually and functionally distinct from the main platform. Animations, transitions, and UI elements are cohesive within the School System but differ in color, layout, and style from the main 12Digits interface. Exiting the School System smoothly returns users to the main platform without disrupting account or classroom data.

Overall, the 12Digits School System is a comprehensive, immersive, and gamified learning environment, combining classroom economy simulations, teacher dashboards, student dashboards, interactive lessons, Fun Zone mini-games, age-adapted learning, leaderboards, and dynamic rewards. The system should feel like a separate world for schools — visually engaging, highly interactive, and more fun and educational than existing platforms like Banqer.
