12Digits School System – Ultimate All-in-One Replit Coding Instructions (With Age Groups)

Goal: Build a fully interactive, gamified financial education platform for schools. Features include onboarding, school payment portal, classroom economy, dashboards, gamification, Fun Zone mini-games, analytics, notifications, teacher-student collaboration, and age-group systems (primary, intermediate, high school).

1. Animated Onboarding & School Portal

Full-screen onboarding for teachers.

Smooth scroll animations: fade-ins, slide-ins, parallax.

Feature showcases:

Custom School Payment / Quote Portal:

Inputs: school name, admin name, email, plan selection.

2. Age-Group Systems

Schools choose an age group during setup:

Primary (ages 6–10):

Simple financial concepts: coins, basic saving/spending.

Fun graphics, colorful dashboards, cute animations.

Mini-games: coin sorting, simple budgeting, interactive quizzes with rewards.

Short lessons with visual storytelling and gamified exercises.

Intermediate (ages 11–13):

Moderate financial concepts: saving, investing, basic stock ideas.

Dashboards include progress bars and simple portfolio simulations.

Mini-games: group investment challenges, market prediction, strategy puzzles.

Lessons include interactive quizzes with scoring.

High School (ages 14–18):

Advanced financial concepts: diversification, risk, investing, simulated markets.

Dashboards: full portfolio simulation, leaderboards, analytics.

Mini-games: team investment challenge, market prediction, strategy puzzles, bonus challenge room.

Lessons include interactive quizzes and scenario-based assignments.

Each age group’s UI, gamification, and difficulty adjust automatically based on selection.

3. Classroom Economy & Interactive Learning

Teacher & student dashboards:

Teachers: create challenges, assign tasks, trigger classroom market events (booms, crashes, custom news).

Students: earn virtual income, manage bank balances, invest in simulated markets, make spending decisions.

Real-time updates and animations for portfolios, leaderboards, and student progress.

Notifications: price alerts, events, achievements, new assignments.
